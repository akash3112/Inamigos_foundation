    Our Story

    About InAmigos Foundation

    A registered, certified, and ISO-qualified non-profit committed to bridging societal gaps through six impactful, sustained initiatives.

    GS

    Mr. Govind Shukla

    Founder & CEO

    Founded on September 23, 2020, InAmigos Foundation reflects Mr. Shukla's vision of uniting minds for lasting societal change. From Chhattisgarh, the organization has grown into a nationally recognized institution with 11,000+ associated members and a strong network of volunteers, professionals, and corporate partners.

    Our Vision

    Collective Action for a Compassionate Society

    At InAmigos Foundation, we believe every contribution - no matter how small - creates ripples of change. Together, we work to build a more inclusive, equitable, and empowered India.

    Milestones

    Our Journey So Far

    2020 - Founded

    InAmigos Foundation established by Mr. Govind Shukla in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh on September 23.

    2021-22 - Six Projects Launched

    Seva, Bachpanshala, Jeev, Udaan, Prakriti, and Vikas launched across Chhattisgarh and beyond.

    2023 - National Scale

    Reached 100,000+ applicants, empowered 900+ women, and planted 20,000+ trees nationwide. ISO 9001:2015 certification awarded.

    2024-25 - 11,000+ Member Community

    NITI Aayog registered, 250+ interns joined permanently. CSR-1 registration enables corporate collaboration at scale.
